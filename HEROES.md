# Heroes of Cavaleri

64 unique heroes — 2 classes per faction, 4 named heroes per class.

**Stat growth per level:**
- Might classes: Attack +2, Defense +2, Spell Power +1, Knowledge +1
- Magic classes: Attack +1, Defense +1, Spell Power +2, Knowledge +2

---

## Castle

### Knight (Might)

- **Sir Galahad** — A veteran crusader who led the siege of Thornwall Keep. His unwavering discipline inspires even the most reluctant conscript to hold the line.
- **Lord Haart** — Once a noble of the northern provinces, he traded courtly life for the battlefield. His tactical mind has turned the tide of countless skirmishes.
- **Sorsha** — A former captain of the Queen's Guard who earned her rank through sheer combat prowess. She favors swift cavalry charges that shatter enemy formations.
- **Tyris** — A knight-errant who wanders the realm seeking worthy causes. Her legendary strength with a two-handed sword has become the stuff of tavern songs.

### Cleric (Magic)

- **Adela** — A healer from the Cathedral of Light who took up arms when undead legions threatened her parish. Her blessings can mend wounds that would fell lesser warriors.
- **Loynis** — An aging priest whose quiet demeanor hides a fierce devotion to the living. He channels divine wrath against the unholy with devastating precision.
- **Caitlin** — Raised in an orphanage run by warrior-monks, she learned both scripture and swordplay. Her protective wards have saved entire battalions from arcane assault.
- **Rion** — The youngest cleric to ever receive the Silver Mantle, his prodigious talent for restorative magic is matched only by his reckless courage in battle.

## Necropolis

### Death Knight (Might)

- **Lord Haart** — A fallen paladin who was resurrected against his will and now serves the lords of death. Fragments of his former honor still surface in his grim command style.
- **Tamika** — A once-proud general who chose undeath over defeat. She drills her skeletal legions with the same iron discipline she demanded of the living.
- **Straker** — A mercenary who bartered his soul for immortality and received more than he bargained for. His rage on the battlefield is fueled by centuries of regret.
- **Clavius** — An ancient warlord entombed for millennia, recently unearthed by necromantic ritual. His archaic battle tactics prove devastatingly effective against modern armies.

### Necromancer (Magic)

- **Sandro** — A lich of terrifying intellect who orchestrates wars from the shadows. His mastery of death magic is rivaled by none among the living or dead.
- **Vidomina** — Once a promising student of the Tower academies, she was expelled for forbidden experiments. She now raises the dead with an artist's precision.
- **Thant** — A hermit necromancer who communes with spirits in the deep crypts. His understanding of the boundary between life and death grants him unnatural power.
- **Isra** — A former midwife who discovered her gift for necromancy after a plague took her village. She views raising the dead as simply another form of healing.

## Rampart

### Ranger (Might)

- **Jenova** — A half-elf scout who patrols the ancient forest borders. Her knowledge of woodland warfare makes her forces nearly invisible among the trees.
- **Ryland** — The son of a centaur chieftain and a human diplomat, he bridges two worlds. His mounted archers strike with the speed and precision of a summer storm.
- **Mephala** — A former elven assassin who now leads guerrilla forces against deforestation campaigns. No army has ever successfully marched through her territory.
- **Gelu** — A legendary marksman whose arrows are said to never miss. He fights to preserve the balance between civilization and the wild.

### Druid (Magic)

- **Elleshar** — An ancient tree-speaker who has communed with the forest for centuries. The roots and branches themselves answer his call in times of war.
- **Gem** — A wandering herbalist who discovered she could command the very essence of nature. Her healing groves have become sanctuaries for the wounded of all factions.
- **Aeris** — A storm-caller who channels the fury of wind and rain against those who threaten the woodlands. Her tempests have scattered armies twice the size of her own.
- **Coronius** — A druid philosopher who believes conflict is a natural cycle, like seasons. He wields nature's power with detached precision, viewing battle as an ecosystem to manage.

## Tower

### Alchemist (Might)

- **Fafner** — A master of transmutation who forges enchanted armor and weapons for his troops. His golems march in perfect formation, tireless and obedient.
- **Neela** — A former mining engineer who discovered alchemical secrets deep underground. She fortifies positions with construct guardians that never sleep or falter.
- **Josephine** — A noblewoman-turned-inventor whose mechanical war machines terrify conventional armies. Her battlefield innovations blur the line between science and magic.
- **Torosar** — An elderly craftsman whose homunculi serve as both scouts and soldiers. His meticulous approach to warfare leaves nothing to chance.

### Wizard (Magic)

- **Solmyr** — A djinn-born sorcerer whose lightning strikes are legendary across the realm. He serves the Tower not out of loyalty, but for access to its vast arcane libraries.
- **Cyra** — A prodigy of elemental magic who graduated from the Academy at half the usual age. Her fireballs are precise enough to strike a single soldier in a crowded melee.
- **Iona** — A planar scholar who draws power from dimensional rifts. Her summoned creatures fight with an alien ferocity that unnerves even seasoned warriors.
- **Theodorus** — A battle-mage veteran who has perfected the art of magical artillery. Entire formations dissolve under his coordinated arcane bombardments.

## Inferno

### Demoniac (Might)

- **Calh** — A pit lord who clawed his way to command through brute force and cunning. His demonic troops fear him more than any mortal enemy.
- **Nymus** — A succubus warlord who prefers deception but excels at direct confrontation. Her armies strike from unexpected angles with infernal coordination.
- **Rashka** — An efreet commander whose fiery temper matches his elemental nature. He burns a path through enemy lines with sadistic glee.
- **Xeron** — A devil prince exiled from the deepest pits for excessive ambition. He leads his forces with cold, calculating brutality.

### Heretic (Magic)

- **Ash** — A human cultist who willingly embraced infernal power for revenge against the kingdom that wronged him. His hellfire incantations leave nothing but cinders.
- **Zydar** — A former monk who lost his faith and found darker patrons. He channels demonic energy through corrupted prayer, twisting holy rites into weapons.
- **Axsis** — A tiefling sorcerer born in the borderlands between worlds. She weaves chaos magic with instinctive ease, as natural to her as breathing.
- **Olema** — An apostate priestess who discovered that infernal pacts grant power faster than divine devotion. Her curses sap the will of entire regiments.

## Dungeon

### Overlord (Might)

- **Gunnar** — A minotaur warlord who rules through strength and brutal pragmatism. His underground fortifications are considered impregnable by surface tacticians.
- **Lorelei** — A dark elf matriarch who commands absolute loyalty through a mix of fear and reward. Her spider cavalry terrifies all who face them.
- **Mutare** — A shapeshifter of unknown origin who has infiltrated and toppled kingdoms from within. On the battlefield, she becomes whatever her army needs most.
- **Shakti** — A troglodyte queen who united the deep clans through conquest. Her ambushes from below have swallowed entire surface expeditions without a trace.

### Warlock (Magic)

- **Alamar** — A dragon-blooded sorcerer whose magical experiments have reshaped entire cavern systems. His scrying abilities ensure he is never caught off guard.
- **Jeddite** — A lich-like warlock who has extended his life through forbidden pacts with subterranean entities. His psychic assaults crush minds before blades are drawn.
- **Deemer** — A blind oracle who sees through magical sight far beyond mortal perception. Her meteor showers are aimed with uncanny accuracy despite her affliction.
- **Malekith** — A dark elf archmage exiled from his own house for practicing magic deemed too dangerous. The dungeon depths suit his research perfectly.

## Stronghold

### Barbarian (Might)

- **Crag Hack** — A legendary berserker whose name alone causes lesser foes to flee. He fights at the front of every charge, his battle-axe carving a path of destruction.
- **Gretchin** — A goblin tactician who compensates for her small stature with overwhelming cunning. Her ambush strategies have humbled many an overconfident knight.
- **Shiva** — A frost giant chieftain who descended from the mountains to conquer the lowlands. Her war hammer shatters shields and morale in equal measure.
- **Tyraxor** — An ogre warlord who united the steppe tribes through single combat. His brutal simplicity in battle is deceptively effective against sophisticated foes.

### Battle Mage (Magic)

- **Gird** — A shaman who channels ancestral spirits to enhance his warriors' strength. His war chants drive troops into a focused fury that overwhelms disciplined formations.
- **Oris** — A cyclops sage who learned magic by studying the stars from mountain peaks. His elemental blasts are crude but devastatingly powerful.
- **Saurug** — A lizardfolk mystic adopted by the steppe clans after wandering from the swamps. His unusual combination of primal and arcane magic bewilders enemy casters.
- **Dessa** — A human witch-doctor who chose the freedom of the steppes over civilization's constraints. She hexes enemy armies from afar, sowing confusion before the charge.

## Fortress

### Beastmaster (Might)

- **Bron** — A gnoll pack-leader who tamed the great swamp wyrms. His bond with reptilian beasts gives his forces a terrifying edge in marshland warfare.
- **Drakon** — A lizardfolk champion whose scales are as tough as plate armor. He leads amphibious assaults that strike from waterways no army expects.
- **Tazar** — A human ranger who grew up among the swamp folk after being abandoned as an infant. He knows every hidden path through the treacherous bogs.
- **Wystan** — A grizzled trapper who turned his knowledge of swamp predators into military strategy. His defensive positions use the terrain as a weapon.

### Witch (Magic)

- **Mirlanda** — A bog-witch whose potions can heal or destroy with equal potency. She brews tactical advantages from the rare herbs that grow only in her swamps.
- **Rosic** — A fungal mage who communicates through the vast mycelium networks beneath the marsh. Her spore clouds disorient and weaken invading forces.
- **Voy** — An ancient crone who has outlived empires through dark bargains with swamp spirits. Her curses are patient, creeping things that erode armies over days.
- **Verdish** — A young prodigy who discovered she could command the venomous creatures of the wetlands. Her swarms of poisonous insects are a nightmare for heavily armored foes.
